Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Ahad Reports Bot is a personal automation tool that publishes video content to Pinterest on behalf of the account owner. It does not collect, store, or share any personal data from third parties.

Data We Access

The app accesses only the Pinterest account of the owner to create and manage pins. No user data is collected from viewers or followers.

Data Storage

No personal data is stored on external servers. All credentials are stored locally on the developer's own machine.
